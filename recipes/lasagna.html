Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Bolognese dish. As a result, it uses Ragù alla Bolognese, which takes a very long time to took. But hey, that means it’s extra good!

Ingredients

  • Ragù alla Bolognese
  • Dried pasta sheets
  • etc.

Instructions

  1. Make layers of sauce, bechamel, cheese, and ragù.
  2. Cook in the oven.
